Hi, I'm Vikram Saraph! I am a researcher and software engineer at the [Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Lab](https://www.jhuapl.edu/). I have a PhD in Computer Science and Masters in pure mathematics from Brown University, and a bachelors degree in the same two fields from the University of Notre Dame. My interests span anything related to applied math or computer science. Broadly speaking, this includes applied cryptography (to security or blockchains), deep learning (both the mathematics of it as well as how to apply it), and problems that emerge at scale (such as in large-scale distributed systems, or in social networks).

I use this space primarily as a learning blog. And since posting [wrong things on the internet](https://xkcd.com/386/) is a great way to get [right answers](https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Cunningham%27s_Law), feel free to reach out to me with any corrections.
