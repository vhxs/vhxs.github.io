Hi, I'm Vikram Saraph! I'm into anything that's related to math, software, computers, or data. I am currently an applied researcher and software engineer at the [Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Lab](https://www.jhuapl.edu/). Prior to this, I was a research scientist at [Facebook AI Research](https://ai.facebook.com/). And before that, I completed a PhD in computer science and a masters in mathematics at Brown University, and I hold a bachelors degree in the same two fields from the University of Notre Dame.

I want to use this space as a form of public note taking to help hammer home concepts that I learn about. As with any note taking, this blog's primary purpose is for me to be able to reference my own thoughts at a later time, so it'll always be a continuous work in progress, though I hope that anyone else reading this may benefit from what I may write down.
